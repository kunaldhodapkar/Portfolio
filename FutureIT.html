In 2018, enterprise-apps market revenue shattered all records— totaling $326 Billion worldwide. But today, when enterprises move at breakneck speed, why even the smallest update in the app takes months to publish?

I was part of an ambitious project which aimed at reducing this deployment time from months... to minutes

What goes on behind-the-scenes?

Businesses store critical data in "systems of record", and...

Businesses have come a long way from manual bookkeeping of business records to local storage in computer hard-drives. Today they store their critical business data (finance, HR, customer) in cloud platforms such as Salesforce, SAP, Mendix etc (called, Systems of record).


These "systems" are (super-)hard to use, so...

However, as much as these Systems of record are valued for their ability to generate reports and insight for the company’s management team, they still remain complicated for the workers who engage with the customers on a regular basis, and often require years of expertise.


Businesses build custom workflow apps, which require IT support & money, so...

Due to complexity of Systems of record, businesses often have to develop their own workflow applications which are poor in usability and incur high developmental costs and long maintenance and deployment cycles.


They need a platform to build custom apps, (super-)fast

There is a dire need of a platform which integrates itself with different Systems of record, helps businesses to create custom workflow apps rapidly and provides a seamless experience to company workers to engage with the customers effectively.


My Role

I led the UX design effort for the entire platform for a duration of 10 months. I regularly collaborated with the 2 visual designers to deliver final designs.

In addition, I worked alongside PMs, QA, Management & Dev team at the client side to deliver mockups, wireframes & prototypes.

My background in software development helped me understand the technical constraints and provide meaningful designs with ready-to-go market attributes.

The platform was launched globally on January 5th 2019.

Knowing our users

Detailed discussions with clients helped us come up with 2 distinct user-personas


App admins

They have critical role of keeping the applications in the organisation running. They install, update, diagnose, and support applications acquired either from a third-party vendor or from an internal development team.

Field workers

They are the users of these workflow applications. They interact with customers and internal stakeholders on a daily basis. They require these apps to be high in usability and rely on them to get their job done.
